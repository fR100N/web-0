Do you know about Gran Canaria ?

On this page we will show you the best trails on the island so that you can get to know it better and spend some good holidays visiting new places with views that will leave you impressed.

- Harold V. Melchert

In the mountains you always have to be careful so we will give you some important points to do trekking in Gran Canaria:

  • The emergency number is 1-1-2.
  • The island is very sunny, take sun cream.
  • Camping is prohibited in many areas.
  • Wear appropriate clothing and footwear.
  • Bring GPS or something with which to orient yourself.
  • Bring enough food and water for the journey.

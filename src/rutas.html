Circular route in the Roque Nublo

Circular path of easy difficulty that starts and ends in the Llanos de la Pez roasting area. The most demanding part of the route is the ascent to the Degollada de Piedras Blancas, near the peak of Las Nieves. You can get good views of Roque Nublo and Amurga.

Tunte - Cruz de Tejeda

A linear path of moderate difficulty and with a very historical character, since it allows you to visit places such as the town of Tunte (the largest set of natural shelters of the pre-Hispanic population of the Canary Islands) and part of the La Plata path, a centennial road of communication between the southern midlands and the central summit.

Cruz de Tejeda - Gáldar

Along the way there are only two places where we can get a bar at Lomo del Palo and Saucillo. Ideal to make it a clear day of autumn, winter or spring. We made a small detour that is worthwhile to appreciate the Caldera de los PInos de Galdar. The GPS is recommended because there are very few signs to show us the way.
